About This Page

Hi my name is Dokza, here i will tell my story journey in S&P. on august 20, 2024 an innocent boy who had graduated from college began his journey in Jakarta, a city where he had never been, a city that had not been recognized, a city where the author had no relatives. The author came to the office commanded by Mr. Ezra and Mr. Hans as S&P's tax partner located in Cempaka Putih and was warmly welcomed by the team. the author was very able to interact and there were no boundaries between new people and old people, ahh so relieved I had a second family overseas. the journey began, the author gained a lot of knowledge starting from making invoices, procedures for preparing income tax and VAT, cases, and how to meet and talk with clients. The author is very happy that a new boy who has just entered is very appreciated when he graduates, it feels like a song whose lyrics are “Semua Aku Dirayakan.” The author also made several visits both in the North Jakarta, West Jakarta, Bandung, and others. the author is also very happy in January to be able to travel to Bandung with the team where the event was cool and very fun. ah there are still many stories that I may not be able to express one by one at this time. in the end the author would like to thank S&P and Mr. Ezra as managing partner along with Mr. Hans and all the team at S&P, I would like to thank you very much for being given the opportunity and trust to be able to take part in working here. Thank you
